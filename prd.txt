ğŸ”¹ Feature 2: Damage Detection Model

Detect damaged car parts and severity.

YOLOv8 pretrained model (fine-tuned if possible)

The model classifies damage into 4 vehicle zones that together cover the entire vehicle:

2. Rear (rear bumper, trunk, taillights, rear windshield)
3. Left Side (left doors, left fender, left mirror, left quarter panel)

Zone name (Front / Rear / Left Side / Right Side)

Bounding box

Severity classification (minor/moderate/severe)
